Calories & Macros Calculator

Welcome to your personalized fitness calculator. Whether your goal is fat loss, muscle gain, or maintaining a healthy body, it all starts with understanding your daily calorie needs and macronutrient breakdown.
This tool will help you calculate:
✅ Maintenance Calories (to maintain your weight)
✅ Cutting Calories & Macros (to lose fat)
✅ Bulking Calories & Macros (to gain lean muscle)

⚙️ How It Works:

Enter your gender, age, height, weight, and activity level. The calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to estimate your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) and multiplies it by your activity level to get your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure). You’ll instantly get:
✅ Calories for maintenance, cutting, and bulking
✅ Macronutrients (Protein, Fats, Carbs) tailored for your goal

🧠 Why Track Calories & Macros?

You can’t manage what you don’t measure.
Understanding your calorie intake helps you:
Avoid random guessing or extreme diets
Eat enough to build muscle or preserve it during fat loss
Customize your nutrition around your training
