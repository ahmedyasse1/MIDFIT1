Nutrition Follow-UP

Medical-Backed Nutrition for Real, Sustainable Results This premium package combines fitness-focused meal planning with the expertise of a certified nutrition doctor, ensuring your plan is not just effective — but also medically appropriate, safe, and sustainable.

What You Get:

✅ Initial Medical Nutrition Consultation (via Doctor)
– Full assessment of health status, goals, and possible limitations – Bloodwork or labs can be reviewed if needed
✅ Customized Meal Plan (Doctor + Coach Approved)
– Tailored to your goals (fat loss, muscle gain, PCOS, etc.) – Includes Egyptian/local food options – Adjusted for medical conditions if present (thyroid, insulin resistance, etc.)
✅ Bi-Weekly Follow-Up with the Doctor & Coach
– Progress check (weight, photos, symptoms, energy, appetite, etc.) – Adjustments to the plan – Ongoing WhatsApp support from both coach and doctor
✅ Educational Materials
– PDF guides for portion control, label reading, and smart shopping – Optional supplement recommendations (if approved by doctor) – One-on-one session with a certified doctor or licensed dietitian
