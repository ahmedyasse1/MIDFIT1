My Steps For Program Design

How We Put An
Excellent Program Design

How We Put Together an Excellent Program From consultation to follow-up, each step is tailored to you. We assess, plan, and adapt — to deliver real, lasting results.

Initial Consultation & Setting Fitness Goals

Understand client goals (fat loss, muscle gain, performance, rehab, etc.) Discuss medical history, injuries, lifestyle, time availability, and training background Set realistic short-term and long-term goals

Physical & Health Assessment

Health and lifestyle questionnaires (PAR-Q) Nutritional habits and sleep patterns Readiness for change and commitment level Body composition (weight, body fat %, measurements) Posture and mobility screening Movement assessment (e.g. squat, push-up form)Strength and endurance testing (e.g. 1RM estimate, plank hold, etc.)

Program Planning & Design

Choose appropriate training split and structure (e.g. full body, push/pull/legs) Select exercises based on client level and limitations Determine training volume, intensity, rest, and frequency Incorporate periodization if needed (phased progression)
